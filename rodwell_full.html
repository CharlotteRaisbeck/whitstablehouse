Care Suites for People with Nursing Needs

Rodwell House is a purpose built home in extensive and peaceful grounds in Addlestone, Surrey. The team are well-equipped to deliver 24-hour nursing care in high quality, affordable accommodation. We cater to a variety of care needs, both physical frailty and dementia type illnesses.

Care Suites

The Home consists solely of Care Suites. These are larger rooms than those found in traditional nursing homes, designed with residents and their families in mind. Each room has its own living room and kitchen space, encouraging independence as well as family and friends’ visits.

Read more about our Care Suites here.

Relationship Centred Care™

We believe it is important to understand each resident’s past and present relationships, empowering them to talk about their memories, to give them a chance to be heard and feel valued. We pride ourselves on providing Relationship Centred Care™ which puts each resident at the heart of a mutually beneficial relationship between their family and friends, carers and healthcare professionals.

Read more about Relationship Centred Care™ here.

Digitally Transformed Care

We utilise digital technologies to personalise our residents’ care. Always placing the resident first, we’re use remote monitoring (only with granted consent) to “end the round” – enabling staff to support residents only when they need to, as opposed to through routinised, institutional checks.

Read more about digitally transformed care here.
